Help & Support

Galactic Reads is your ultimate resource for exploring the universe of books. Whether you're a fan of science fiction, fantasy, or any other genre, Galactic Reads helps you find, sort, and manage your book collection.

This help section will guide you through the key features of the app, answer frequently asked questions, and provide troubleshooting tips.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the "Sign Up" button on the login page and fill in the required fields to create your account.

Q2: How can I search for books?

A: Use the search bar at the top of the main page to search by title, author, or keyword. You can also filter books by universe or genre using the toolbar.

Q3: How do I add a new book?

A: Currently, Galactic Reads does not support manual book entry. However, you can explore thousands of books available in the database by using the filtering options.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues, here are some common problems and solutions:

  • Can't Log In: Make sure your username and password are correct. If you’ve forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.
  • Books Are Not Showing: Check that you’ve selected the correct universe or genre filters. If there are no books, try adjusting your filters.
  • App Not Responding: Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, check for updates or contact support.
